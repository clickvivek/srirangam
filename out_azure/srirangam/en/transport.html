Transport Information

How to Reach Srirangam

Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple is located on Srirangam Island in Tiruchirappalli (Trichy), Tamil Nadu. The temple is well connected by air, rail and road, making it easy for devotees from across India and abroad to visit.

Local Bus Stands

From Chathiram Bus Stand

~5 km⏱ 15–20 mins
  • Frequent city buses to Srirangam.
  • Auto-rickshaws readily available.
  • Taxis and app-based cabs offer direct drop.

From Trichy Central Bus Stand

~10 km⏱ 25–35 mins
  • City buses operate regularly.
  • Autos and taxis available outside terminal.
  • Recommended for families travelling with luggage.

From Panjapur Integrated Terminus

~15–18 km⏱ 30–45 mins
  • Direct or connecting buses.
  • Allow extra time during peak traffic.

Railway Stations

Srirangam Railway Station

~1.5 km from the temple. Easily accessible by walk, auto or taxi.

Tiruchirappalli Junction

~10 km from the temple. City buses, autos and taxis available outside.

By Air

~15 km to Airport⏱ 30–40 mins travel

Trichy International Airport has domestic connectivity to Chennai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Delhi and Kochi, along with international flights to Singapore, Kuala Lumpur, Colombo and Gulf destinations.

  • 24×7 prepaid taxis & App-based cabs.
  • Airport buses to city with Srirangam connections.

Parking Information

Dedicated parking is available around the temple. During weekends and major festivals (like Vaikunta Ekadasi), arrive early as parking fills quickly.

Travel Tips

  • Arrive at least 30 minutes before the queue closing time.
  • Expect additional traffic during festivals.
  • If your train stops at Srirangam Station, alight there for the shortest transfer.
  • Carry drinking water and small cash for local transport.
  • Early morning visits are recommended during summer.
